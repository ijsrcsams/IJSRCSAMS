Welcome to IJCS

The International Journal of Computer Science (IJCS) is a scholarly peer-reviewed journal. It aims to provide an international forum for scientists and engineers to exchange new ideas and findings in the field of computer science.

IJCS is published by the International Association of Engineers (IAENG).

About IJCS

The International Journal of Computer Science (IJCS) is a quarterly peer-reviewed journal. It covers all aspects of computer science, including but not limited to algorithms, artificial intelligence, computer architecture, computer graphics, computer networks, data structures, databases, distributed systems, information security, machine learning, operating systems, programming languages, software engineering, and theoretical computer science.
